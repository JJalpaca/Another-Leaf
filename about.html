ABOUT US



Origins

Another Leaf began with three friends on a late night walk, talking about their feelings and their current experiences. Little did they know that talking comfortably as friends will give birth to a project that will affect millions of young people nationwide.

Through the Enactus society, they grew their project, going through change after change to finally find the perfect outlet for their passion. Podcasts. As they continue to grow their project, their hearts and minds will be filled with many lessons throughout the process.

Join us as we embark on this journey of mental health and well-being.


Our Mission

Oxford English Dictionary “the state of being healthy, comfortable and happy” This is the Oxford Dictionary definition of what personal well being, however, we know that personal well being is more than this. It’s about adding value to your day to day and is subjective to the individual. We all have different goals, personalities and ambitions. The Philosopher Aristotle revealed that as well as personal well being about happiness, it includes the fulfilment of long term goals - which is a concept that Another Leaf will help young people prepare for through the creation of well being podcasts.

Our mission and livelihood is to make personal well-being an integral part of young people's lives in order to tackle the prevailing issue of poor mental health in young people. This helps achieve the UN Sustainable Development goal of Good Health and Well Being.

Another Leaf, want to take a life course approach intervening early to address neglected determinants that would increase the risk of mental health problems from escalating.
